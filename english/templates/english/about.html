Who We Are?

We are a group of passionate students and supportive mentors who believe that language is more than just words — it’s a way to connect, express, and grow. The English Club is led by experienced upper-year students who are always ready to guide and help you improve your English in a fun and stress-free environment. We come from different backgrounds, majors — but we’re united by a shared goal: to create a welcoming space where everyone feels comfortable speaking and learning English.
